what is democracy have you ever wondered how the people in a country get to decide who their leaders are and what rules they should follow this is all about democracy democracy is a system where the citizens of a country have the power to choose their leaders and influence the decisions made by the government the word democracy comes from ancient Greek meaning citizens power let's go back in time to see where this idea started in ient Athens citizens would Gather in an assembly to discuss and decide on laws and other important matters however it wasn't perfect only men who were citizens could vote while women and slaves were excluded fast forward to the 18th century the French Revolution marked a significant Turning Point French citizens overthrew the absolute monarchy and for the first time the idea of universal suffrage emerged this meant that any citizen regardless of wealth could vote however women still couldn't vote at this time over time Democratic ideas spread to many other countries today democracy is based on the principle that everyone has equal rights and duties it's about respecting each other and ensuring that everyone has a say in how the country is run in a democratic system citizens vote to elect their leaders and participate in the decision- making process this is done through elections where people choose their representatives the government is accountable to the people and the rule of law ensures that everyone is treated fairly civic education plays a crucial role in sustaining democracy it teaches citizens about their rights responsibilities and how to participate in the Democratic process this education helps people understand the complexities of political life and how to make informed decisions in summary democracy is about giving power to the people ensuring everyone's voice is heard and maintaining a fair and just Society it's a system that has evolved over centuries and continues to be refined to ensure equality and justice for all
